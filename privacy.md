# Sotto — Privacy Policy
_Last updated: July 10, 2026_

Sotto is built to keep your words on your device.

**Translation is on-device.** The texts you type, dictate, photograph, or
hear translated are processed entirely on your iPhone/iPad by a locally
installed AI model. They are never sent to us or to any server, and we
cannot see them. Your history and favorites are stored only on your device.

**No accounts, no analytics, no ads.** Sotto has no sign-up, collects no
usage analytics, and contains no advertising or tracking SDKs.

**Speech.** Speech recognition and voice output use Apple's system
frameworks. On-device recognition is used whenever iOS supports it for the
language. If you enable "Allow online fallback" in Settings, audio for
unsupported languages is processed by Apple's servers under Apple's privacy
terms; turn the toggle off for strictly on-device behavior.

**Model download.** The translation model is downloaded once from Hugging
Face's servers; like any download, that request exposes your IP address to
the hosting provider. No personal data is included.

**Purchases.** Payments are processed by Apple. To validate purchases and
enable features like Restore, Sotto uses RevenueCat, which receives your
App Store receipt and a random anonymous identifier — never your name,
email, or the content you translate. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for
their handling.

**Permissions.** Camera (photograph text to translate), microphone and
speech recognition (dictation and conversation mode) are used only for
those features, only while you use them.
