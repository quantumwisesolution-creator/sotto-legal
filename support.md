# Sotto Support

Sotto is an offline translator for iPhone and iPad.

**Contact:** quantumwise.solution@gmail.com
We answer within a few days.

**Common questions**

- **Does Sotto need internet?** Only once, to download the translation
  model (about 2.5 GB, Wi-Fi recommended). All translation afterwards
  happens on your device.
- **Where is my data stored?** On your device. Translations, photos, and
  conversations are never uploaded anywhere.
- **How do I restore my purchase?** Settings tab, Advanced section,
  "Restore Purchases".
- **A language is missing voice input or speech.** Voice features depend
  on what iOS supports for that language; text translation still works.
